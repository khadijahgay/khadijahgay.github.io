FAQ


Answers to frequently asked questions:



If you have a question not listed here, email me at contact@khadijahgay.site or go to request a consultation and include your question in the message.


What services do you offer?

I offer website design, website development, website management, website advisement, app management, and app advisement services! You can go to my Services Page to order and pay for a service.


Can I design my own website?

Of course! I am a web developer first and foremost. If you want to design your look for your website, I can just make it look how you would like it. It's not required that I design it for you.


How do you create my website?

I can use a website builder such as Wordpress, Wix, Squarespace, Godaddy, Weebly, and more! I can also code your website from scratch using a combination of coding languages. Just include which builder you would like to use or state that you want it built from scratch when you request a consultation.


What is a website builder?

A website builder is an app or website that creates a website for you, rather than coding a website from scratch.


What coding languages do you know?

I know HTML, Java, CSS, Javascript, PHP, and SQL. I use all or a combination of these when I code your website.


What is the website building process like?

First, you request a consultation. Please include as many details as possible within your request, it will make the process faster.
After you request, I will respond within 2-3 business days. When I respond, we will do an interview. The interview process is me asking
questions to get a feel for what you would want your website to look like and how you want the website built. You will also have the opportunity to ask me any questions.
After the interview, I do a mock-up of your website and ask you to approve it. I will continue to make changes until you're happy with it. Once approved,
I will start making the website. After we complete the website, you will do a final review of it. As long as you're happy with the results, you or I will publish it,
depending on what package you wanted. If you aren't happy, we continue to make changes until you are.


How long does website building take?

It depends on how many pages you have on your site and how complex they are. A page is every section of the website that your clients can view.
If you have multiple pages, it will take longer. On average, it takes 3-7 days to make a page. Simple websites with 1-5 pages take around a month to complete. Contact me for a more specific estimate.


How many changes can I make to my website?

It depends on the package you select. I can manage your website for a fee monthly, or I can make the website and you handle the rest.
Changes depend on price, the more you pay, the more changes you can make per month. I understand it may not be ideal to have a limit,
but in order to ensure that your needs are met, I have to limit changes to effectively use my time.


Can you help me run my website?

Yes! I understand that not many have knowledge of website servers and document uploads. I do teach my clients how to use their apps for a fee,
manage the apps for you, or you can handle it yourself. It all depends on what you would like.


Do I have to pay to contact you?

Of course not. All consultations are free. It's easier to simply email me, but you can do that or request a consultation.
If you decide not to purchase a service from me, that is okay too. Although, I do hope I can meet your needs!
